welcome to Freezcool Technical LLC

Freezcool Technical LLC was formed by a pioneer in the development and manufacturing of walk-in cold rooms. The incorporated ideas in building technologies as well as refrigeration to build unique, one-piece walk-in units for the cold room industry.
Today, Freezcool leads the cold room industry with its diverse line of products and the ability to design, manufacture, delive and install custom walk-in units, for indoor and outdoor use.
Freezcool supplies a full line of refrigerations systems; including self-contained, remote pre-assembled and rack integrated units.
The most important factor in determining the success of the cold store is the proper installation of the panels, construction, electrical systems and genuine refrigeration equipment and accessories. At Freezcool Technical LLC,We guarantee a whole lot than that!

Products & Services

Walk-in Chillers & Freezers

Freezcool Tech LLC offers energy-efficient walk-in units for indoor/outdoor storage. The one-piece outdoor design saves construction costs and reduces overall building footprint. Used by several foodservice chain partners for years.

Industrial Cold Rooms

Freezcool Technical LLC manufactures Industrial cold rooms for wide range of Storage applications such as Food Processing units, Frozen Food Storage, Medical Labs, Dry Food Storage, large scale Distribution and Logistics Centers. We use high quality materials, equipment and accessories to give the best possible results to the end customers.

Portable Skid Mounted Refrigerated Containers

Freezcool Technical LLC built Skid mounted refrigerated containers for portable applications. Different types of cold rooms can be made for different temperatures and sizes and it is being used as portable units which are built on strong base to get good strength.

Blast Chillers & Blast Freezers

Freezcool Technical LLC creates blast freezers & blast chillers for cooling food quickly to a low temperature that is relatively safe from bacterial growth. Blast freezing is a rapid form of freezing which minimizes the damage and preserves the food at a higher quality. They are often used in the food industry for products such as ice cream, ready meals, meat, fish and vegetable products.

Centralized Refrigeration System

Freezcool Technical LLC makes power Pack system for high capacity commercial and industrial cost-effective applications. Our solution has an advanced central monitoring system which allows the clients to control the plant from any part of the world through remote monitoring system. It has a standby capacity, low maintenance, high reliability, high flexibility, lower running cost and thereby lowers power consumption up to 35%.

Cold Room Panels & Doors

We use Eco-friendly, CFC free, Polyurethane insulated panel, which will retain the thermal insulation features for the product storage.Panels are available in different thickness like 50,80,100,120,150 and 200 mm and the various densities. The standard panel width comes 1170mm and length comes up to 12 meters and can be produced according to the project demands. The Panels are neither toxic nor irritating and do not support development of fungi.Wide range of cladding materials is available to manufacture panels.

  • Stucco embossed Aluminium
  • Polyster pre-painted galvanized steel
  • Polyster pre-painted Aluminium
  • Galvanized steel with 200 micron
  • PVC coating system(plastisol)
